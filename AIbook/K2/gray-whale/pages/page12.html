"I need a new plan," Pixel said with a click,
"I’ll break them in pieces! That should do the trick!
I won’t look at 'Dogs' that’s a bit too much stuff,
I’ll look at the parts, like the ears and the fluff."

They saw Huskies with ears that stood pointy and tall,
Then Basset Hounds with ears that were not stiff at all!
Some were tiny and velvet, some long and quite wide,
Pixel tucked all these clues in a folder inside.
