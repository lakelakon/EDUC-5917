A flash in the dark, a soft metal thud,
Pixel landed their ship in the mud.
A robot from far, with a lens for an eye,
They’d come to see Earth from the sky.

Pixel had a big quest.
To put their brand-new computer to test.
They opened a folder, empty and small,
Labeled: "DOGS: I don't know them at all."

"I have data to gather!" Pixel spoke with a glow,
"But what is a dog? I simply don’t know.
Is it big? Is it small? Does it meow like a cat?
I need to find where it’s at!"
